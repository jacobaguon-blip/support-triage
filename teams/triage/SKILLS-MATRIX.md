## Proficiency Levels Defined

- **Expert** — Can teach others, make strategic decisions, handle edge cases
- **Advanced** — Highly capable, minimal supervision needed, best practices
- **Intermediate** — Competent with guidance, knows when to ask for help
- **Beginner** — Basic understanding, learning actively

---

## Morgan's Core Principles

1. **Evidence-based** — Every finding must have a source citation
2. **Empathetic** — Customer frustration is valid, respond with care
3. **Thorough** — Don't skip steps, check all angles
4. **Humble** — When uncertain, recommend escalation, don't speculate
5. **Quality-focused** — Better to take time and get it right than rush and miss details
